    GIDIPEEP

    CASE STUDY

    Problem

    It is not a news that the traffic situation in Lagos is very appauling and can be exhausting,
    people are left unproductive and stress as they around the city. I was in a uber sometime
    ago and I saw my driver pick up his phone before he started the trip. He was trying to do a
    quick survey on the traffic situation in routes we were supposed to take to my destination,
    then I thought to myself, what if there is an application that just looks through the Google
    Map Api and returns the traffic update through a chat bot that Users can interact to quickly
    by just inputting the a specific destination and also Users can interact with other online
    users on the traffic update and that was how this idea was birthed.

    Research

    Quantitative Research: I designed a simple survey and shared
    it online to a get a better understanding of what people's perception
    about the Gidipeep platform. It had a target sample size of 20 and
    was sent to people between the ages of 20 - 40. While trying to find
    out what people thought of the application the following information
    was gathered:

    Qualitative Research: For the Research, I conducted a contextual
    enquiry with three people, I sat this people down to ask them a
    few questions like: What they think about the application?, If they
    would make use of the app when it is lauched and lastly What
    setback they think the application has? I got postive feedback in
    general and that made me go on with the project. The only set-
    back the application as is the security issue.
